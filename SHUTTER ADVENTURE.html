An Adventure of Stories and Snapshots

Participants embark on a journey filled with stories waiting to be uncovered and moments waiting to be captured. The adventure promises not just the thrill of the hunt and the joy of photography but a collection of memories and experiences to cherish forever.
Join us on this unique adventure, where your quest for treasure leads you through a myriad of stories captured in time, each photograph a testament to your journey, and every solved riddle a step closer to uncovering the ultimate prize.

Decipher and Discover

As participants unravel each riddle, they are led to distinct checkpoints. Here, not only is the next clue unveiled, but so too is a fresh photography theme, challenging players to capture their surroundings in a way that reflects this theme.



Dual Challenges

This innovative competition layout is designed to test participants on multiple fronts - their quick-wittedness and speed in solving riddles, alongside their ability to see and capture the world artistically through photography.



Themes as Canvases

every checkpoint, the photography theme serves as a canvas for creativity. Participants must use their lenses to interpret and convey the theme, turning each photograph into a subjective piece of art tied to their adventure.

A Journey of Skills

The event is as much about the journey as it is about the destination. It challenges adventurers to employ both their problem-solving abilities and their creative instincts, making every solved riddle and captured theme a rewarding step closer to the ultimate prize.



Beyond the Obvious

As adventurers progress, they're encouraged to look deeper into their surroundings - to find the extraordinary in the ordinary and capture it, blending the essence of discovery from the treasure hunt with the creative expression of the photography competition.



Memories and Marvels

This dual competition is not just about winning; it's about the experiences gathered, the memories made, and the unique way participants will learn to view the world through their cameras. Each captured image and solved riddle contributes to a treasure trove of personal achievements and memories.
